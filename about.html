About Hayimi Movement

Spreading love and hope to families in need—through compassion, resilience, and community-driven action.

Our Mission

Hayimi Movement is a grassroots initiative rooted in Ethiopia, committed to empowering communities through education, health, and humanitarian support.

What We Do

  • 💛 Supporting Displaced People & Disabilities
  • 🍲 Food Support
  • 📚 School Materials
  • 💊 Life-Saving Medications

Our Vision

A future where every child learns freely, every family thrives, and every community rises with strength.
